# Mafia Game 2026 - Systems Overview

This document provides a high-level overview of all major systems in the game. It is intended to help players and future development.

## Core Loop
- Commit crimes → Earn cash + XP + heat
- Build wealth → Buy properties → Grow drugs / store cars
- Join or create a Family → Donate → Leaders buy Power → Earn hourly pay
- Manage risk (heat, raids, jail, death)
- Progress through ranks and rebirth

## Major Systems

### 1. Crime System
- Street crimes with different risk/reward profiles
- Success chance, XP, cash, heat, health loss
- No XP on failure (as of recent update)
- Cooldowns per crime
- Donator bonus: +25% XP + +20% cash

### 2. Heists
- Larger scale crimes requiring crew
- Higher risk and reward

### 3. Murder / PvP
- Unlocked at Hitman rank + 50% KillSkill
- Clean hits at 75%+ KillSkill
- 60 minute cooldown
- Uses bullets and weapons

### 4. Family System
- Creation cost: 2,000,000 cash or 25 diamonds
- Roles: Boss, Underboss, Accountant, Manager, etc.
- Donation system → Pending Bank
- Power system: Leaders spend family bank to buy Power
- Hourly Pay: 60% to personal bank, 40% cash
- Family power affects hourly payouts and future wars
- VIP buffs available in shop

### 5. Economy & Banking
- Personal Bank (protected money)
- Family Bank + Pending Bank
- Mansion Piggybank (hidden from global leaderboard)
- Property earnings with tax
- Government Fund receives most taxes

### 6. Properties & Safehouse
- House, Villa, Mansion (residential)
- Limits: 1 Mansion, 2 Villas (different cities), 4 Houses (different cities)
- Shed upgrades for weed storage capacity
- Garage for vehicles
- Naming supported
- Tax on purchase and earnings

### 7. Weed Growing
- Separate from regular drug dealing
- Progress 0-5 (water to increase)
- Quality % system (can go negative = destroyed)
- Yield based on quality and property type
- Carry caps based on property + shed level

### 8. Street Dealer
- Buy/sell Coke, Weed, Meth, Pills
- City-based price differences
- Carry caps (Coke 200, Meth 100, Pills 300, Weed 1000)
- Manual amount input

### 9. Vehicle System
- Cars have condition/health
- Tuning, repair, selling, crushing
- Required for racing (min 75% health)
- Categories planned: Low-end, Mid, High, Super Sport

### 10. Racing
- 2-player races only
- Post a race with timer (5min / 15min / 1h / 2h)
- Entry fee + betting
- 10 minute cooldown after racing

### 11. Jail System
- Train breakout experience
- Attempt escape based on skill
- See other inmates

### 12. Death System
- Health can reach 0 → Death for 60 minutes
- Limited access while dead (mainly leaderboard + jail)

### 13. Donator / VIP System
- One-time purchase (500 diamonds)
- Perks: cooldown reduction, golden name, better yields, shop discounts, etc.

### 14. Admin Tools
- Give money
- Tax rate control
- Logbook
- Simulated moderation tools

### 15. Live Trackers
- Global system showing cooldowns, timers, and status
- Should be consistent across the entire game
