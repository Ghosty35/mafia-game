# Street Dealer & Drug Economy

## Drugs
- Coke, Weed, Meth, Pills
- Buy low in one city, sell high in another
- Prices change every 4 hours per city

## Carry Caps (Balance)
- Coke: 200 kg
- Meth: 100 kg (most restricted)
- Pills: 300 kg
- Weed: 1000 kg (higher because of growing)

## Manual Amounts
- Players enter exact amount to buy/sell
- Confirmation before transaction
- 1.5% tax on buys

## Integration with Weed
- Weed from Street Dealer or grown in Safehouse
- Grown weed respects shed caps and quality %

## Balance Notes
- City multipliers create profitable runs
- Caps prevent infinite hoarding
- Tax to Community Fund
