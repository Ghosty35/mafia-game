# Player Progression

## Ranks
- Hobo → Street Rat → Small Time Hustler → Gangster → Made Man → Kingpin
- Based on total wealth

## XP & Leveling
- XP from crimes (no XP on failure)
- Level up gives small bonuses
- Donator +25% XP

## Rebirth
- Unlocked at high level (Godfather area)
- Resets level but gives permanent bonuses
- Costs and benefits scale

## KillSkill (Murder)
- Gained from successful murder training
- Unlocks Murder at 50%
- Clean hits at 75%

## Breakout Skill
- Trained in Jail
- Increases chance to escape jail

---
*Progression should feel rewarding and long-term.*
