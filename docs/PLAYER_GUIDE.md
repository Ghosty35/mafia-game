# Mafia Game 2026 - Player Guide (Friendly Version)

Welcome to the streets. This guide explains the core systems in simple terms so you can survive and thrive.

## Getting Started
- Do low-risk crimes like Pickpocket to build cash and XP.
- Level up by committing crimes.
- Buy a House as soon as possible to start growing Weed.

## Crimes
- Each crime has success chance, reward, and risk (jail, heat, health loss).
- **Important**: You get **no XP** if you fail.
- Higher heat = more chance police will catch you.
- Donators get big bonuses on XP and cash.

**Current Crime Examples** (approximate):
- Pickpocket: Easy, 90% success, small cash, low risk.
- Rob Store: Medium.
- Steal Car: Good for getting vehicles.
- Bank Heist: High risk, high reward.

## Families
- Join or create one (costs 2M cash or 25 diamonds).
- Donate to build the family bank.
- Leaders use bank to buy "Power".
- Power gives everyone in the family hourly pay (60% to bank, 40% cash).
- Formula is roughly: Power/200 + Bank/500,000 per hour per member.

## Safehouses & Properties
- Buy House (cheap, small), Villa, or Mansion (expensive, best).
- Limits: 1 Mansion, max 2 Villas in different cities, max 4 Houses in different cities.
- Each property gives you spots to grow weed and store cars.
- **Mansions have a secret Piggybank** – money hidden from the global leaderboard (but family can see it).

## Weed Growing
- Plant in your property.
- Water multiple times (progress 1/5 to 5/5).
- Each water has chance to boost quality % or lower it.
- Bad quality = less money or destroyed harvest.
- Higher quality = much better payout.

## Banking
- Personal Bank: Safer than carrying cash.
- Family Bank: Shared with family.
- Piggybank (Mansion only): Hidden stash.

**Taxes**:
- 10% when buying property.
- 20% on property earnings.
- Small tax on bank transfers.

## Racing
- Post a race with your car.
- Opponent joins.
- Winner takes all bets.
- Your car needs good condition (repair/tune in Garage).

## Donator Perks
If you buy Donator status:
- More XP and money from crimes.
- Shop discounts.
- Faster cooldowns.
- Golden name.
- And more.

## Important Tips
- Always confirm big money moves.
- Heat is dangerous – keep it low.
- Family power helps everyone earn passively.
- Properties cost money to maintain (bills).

Play smart. The streets don't forgive mistakes.
