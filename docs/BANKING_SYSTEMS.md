# Banking Systems

## Personal Bank
- Protected from crime losses
- Deposit from cash
- Withdraw to cash
- 0.5% tax on transactions (default, admin adjustable)

## Family Bank
- Shared family treasury
- Donations go to Pending first
- Accepted donations move to main bank + give respect
- Used to buy Power

## Mansion Piggybank
- Private hidden bank only for Mansion owners
- Not counted in global leaderboard wealth
- Visible to family members in family leaderboard
- Deposit and withdraw like personal bank

## Tax on Banking
- Bank payments for bills: +5% penalty
- General transactions: 0.5%

## Post Office
- Central place to view and pay:
  - Open bills
  - Weekly tax
  - Unpaid property taxes
- Shows live trackers for each property's earnings and tax rate

---
*All money movement should have clear confirmation and immediate feedback.*
