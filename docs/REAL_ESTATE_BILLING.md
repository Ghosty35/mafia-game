# Real Estate & Billing

## Buying Properties
- Only in current city
- Tax: 10% on purchase price
- Custom naming supported
- Limits: 1 Mansion, 2 Villas (different cities), 4 Houses (different cities), total max 4

## Earnings
- Properties generate hourly income
- 20% tax on earnings
- Weekly earnings tracked per property

## Billing
- Pay maintenance from cash or bank (+5% penalty on bank)
- Autopay option (weekly before Sunday)
- Debt carries over with interest/tax

## Post Office
- View all open bills, weekly tax, unpaid taxes
- Pay individually or all at once
- See each property's current tax rate and earnings

## Government Fund
- Receives purchase tax + earnings tax + billing penalties
- Visible in Admin economy summary

---
*Properties are long-term investments with ongoing costs.*
